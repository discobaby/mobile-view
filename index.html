Britney Love

Hey! I am Ben and I am obsessed with Britney Spears. For as long as I can remember, I have been listening to her songs everyday and they get me through the weeks.

Her music represents everything I love, and it fulfills my pop-star fantasy. I love others like Gaga and Gwen Stefani, but there is something about Britney that makes her stand out from the rest.

She is the human embodiment of good energy- in a world where we do not see much of this. Her smile is infectious and has the kindest soul known to man.
